About Snasa

Anupam Mishra aka Snasa, is a young budding INDIAN DJ /Producer.
At age 7 , he was introduced to music in the form of singing (Indian classical music ).
At age 13 , he heard first EDM track (Martin Garrix - Animals) and then the journey began.
... Age 14 , He made his first ever EDM Track Jack It Up to which he states "The Sounds Were Very Basic And Amateur HAHA(Laughs) . Don,t judge me on that " . He made Tracks After Track on genres Big Room, Future Bass, Progressive House.
Age 15, One Of His Budding Tracks Got Placed As No.1 On Reverbnation Mumbai Charts .
Age 16, First Break Through Track Carnival Went Online and People Instantly Clicked with It and Were Jumping to It . He Played At One Of The Happening Scenes In Mumbai #NotronikBurnInGlow . It Was His Debut Set Remembering Which He States "The Crowd Was Epic , I was so Nervous My Hands Were Shaking Before Pressing the PLAY . I said to Myself that This Is It And Pressed PLAY And The Energy Was SO HIGH , It Was ABOVE THE ROOF". It was a successful event. Recent : He has had Some Amazing Tracks with Aalochak, Rezzr, Bebaak, RaveJaxx. Meri Kismat and Manchale has entered so Many hearts and at the same time Maahol is making people to bang their Bass Up the notch. His music is sure to make you Swing your BODY on the Kick and Bass. His biggest musical influences include Martin Garrix , @Tiësto, Julian Jordan , Jay Hardway , ILLENIUM , Flume . Spread #LOVE and #PEACE.
